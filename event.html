IIB’s Industry Excellence & Leadership Awards 2022

Tuesday, 13th September 2022, Indian Institute of World Culture, 6, B. P. Wadia Road, Basavanagudi, Bangalore, India, PIN: 560004

IIB’s Industry Excellence & Leadership Awards 2022 a grand success, inspiring speeches by business leaders uplift young entrepreneurs. 

Icons of Indian Business, India’s leading business magazine, has been constantly hosting an event every month to award the achievements of promising entrepreneurs while inviting various business leaders to share their experiences with the awardees. The event held at the Indian Institute of World Culture on the 13th of November 2022 concluded successfully.

Industry Excellence & Leadership Awards 2022, organized by IIB witnessed various prominent personalities like Mr. Bhasker Rao IPS (Ex. ADGP, Railways Force, AAP Leader), (Mr. T. K Thimmaraja Setty President, Surabhi Souharda Credit Co-operative Ltd), Dr. M. R. Venkatesh (President, Sri Thyagaraja Co-operative Bank Ltd, Vice-Chairman, BMTC Bangalore), Dr. V. G. Kiran Kumar (Director, Vishal Infrastructure Ltd Chairman, CSR- FKCCI, Lt Governor Rotary Dist 3190), Mr. K. V. Shankar (Senior JDS Leader, Bangalore), Mr. Shivanand Thagadur (President, Karnataka Working Journalist Association, Bangalore), Mr. Sridhar (President, Press Club of Bangalore), Mr. Shashidhar Kote (Actor, Music Director & Familiar Playback Singer), Ln. K. Radhakrishna Holla (President, Karnataka State Travel Operators Association (R)), Mrs. Chhaya Gandhi (President, Karnataka State Chapter, MSME & STARTUPS FORUM, Bengaluru), Dr. Krupakar Narayanaswami (State Award-winning Teacher & Social Worker) and more.

As per Hindu traditions, the lamp was lit and the event kicked off with renowned singer Mohan Krishna's soothing songs that created a pleasant environment for the rest of the event, followed by powerful speeches by the guests, and awards were conferred to the deserving candidates.
