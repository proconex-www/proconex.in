Automation Solutions

Empower your business with state-of-the-art automation solutions tailored to meet the unique requirements of your industry. Our team of experts is dedicated to helping you achieve operational efficiency and cost savings through innovative automation technologies.

Industrial and automation spares encompass a broad range of components and equipment used in industrial settings to ensure the proper functioning and control of machinery and automated systems. Here are some key categories of spares in the realm of industrial and automation systems:

  • PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) Components: PLC CPUs (Central Processing Units), Input/Output (I/O) modules, Communication modules, Power supplies for PLCs
  • HMI (Human-Machine Interface) Components: Touchscreens and display panels, Operator interfaces, Keypads and control panels
  • Industrial Computers and Controllers: Industrial PCs Embedded controllers, Motion controllers
  • Sensors and Transducers: Proximity sensors, Photoelectric sensors, Temperature sensors, Pressure sensors
  • Communication and Networking Equipment: Industrial Ethernet switches, Fieldbus communication modules, Network interface cards
  • Electronic Drives and Motor Controls: Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs), Servo drives, Motor controllers
  • Power Distribution and Protection: Circuit breakers, Fuses and fuse holders, Surge protectors
  • Control Panel Components: Contactors and relays, Timers and counters, Push buttons and indicator lights
