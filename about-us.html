Proconex Private Limited

A Procurement Services company founded in 2019

Proconex Pvt Ltd established in 2019 a dynamic company headquartered in Bangalore, India, proudly standing as a prominent force which has rapidly ascended as one among the top companies in the industrial automation sector and captive power generation in the realm of SUPPLY/SERVICE/REPAIR of Industrial Automation Parts/ Turbine Control Spares.

Proconex takes pride in being at the forefront of the Indian market, offering an extensive range of SUPPLY/SERVICE/REPAIR. It’s core focus lies in Turbine control panels, PLC (Programmable Logic Controller), DCS (Distributed Control System), Excitation systems, LCI (Load Commutated Inverter), Vibration Monitoring System, Drive Controls, VFD (Variable Frequency Drive), and a plethora of other cutting-edge offerings like longevity, extended warranties, etc. Notably, we also excel in providing repairs for obsolete parts, ensuring the performance, longevity and efficiency of CONTROL MODULES, CARDS, POWER SUPPLIES, I/O PACKS, HMIs, PCB (Printed Circuit Boards), etc. For a comprehensive coverage of industrial and automation spares in accordance with industry standards and sometimes surpasing it.

Our offerings encompass products from renowned brands such as GE, SIEMENS, HONEYWELL, ALLEN BRADLEY, ABB, BENTLY NEVADA, GE FANUC, YOKOGAWA, MITSUBISHI, WOODWARD, METSO, OMRON, RUSTRONIC, BAILEY, ALSTOM, FOXBORO, DELTA, etc, irrespective of any make and model.

In our latest initiative, we have expanded our services to supply spares and provide repair solutions beyond Indian borders. Our commitment includes faster turnarounds, economic prices and quality par excellence, supported by an extensive network of trusted and verified vendors, authorized distributors, importers/traders, authorized dealers, stockists, and OEMs.

We specialize in the provision of Control and Instrumentation Spares, offering a comprehensive range that includes DETECTORS, FLOW, LEVEL, TEMPERATURE & PRESSURE MEASURING DEVICES, LIMIT SWITCHES, and more. Our services are customized to meet the needs of major industries, including but not limited to POWER GENERATION (GAS, THERMAL, HYDRO, WIND, AND BIOMASS), PETROCHEMICALS, REFINERIES, MINING, OIL & GAS, STEEL PLANTS, SUGAR PLANTS, PAPER MILLS, SMELTING, AND ALL OTHER METAL WORKING PLANTS, TEXTILE PLANTS, PORTS, AUTOMOBILE PLANTS, PHARMACEUTICALS, AND VARIOUS PROCESSING, AUTOMATION PLANTS, etc in accordance with international standards.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our unique approach to satisfying our clients. Distinguished by our expertise in supplying obsolete and “hard to find parts” or refurbishing, remanufacturing, and repairing existing spare parts, we stand out as a reliable partner in providing top-notch solutions, ensuring with a guarantee for the smooth and optimal performance of PCB boards, Control Cards, Power Cards, IGBT cards, Communication cards, etc of industrial automation spares. Proconex has become synonymous with exceptional service quality, stellar customer service, and a client-driven approach.

Our Motto at Proconex

Is fueled by a vision for success, where each endeavor is a step toward achieving excellence.
