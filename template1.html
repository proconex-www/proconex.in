Our Services for Major Industries

SPARES

We provide extensive support and supply services for both current control systems and instrumentation spares, including obsolete panels that may not be supported by the original equipment manufacturers (OEMs). Our supplied parts come with a 12 to 18-month warranty, ensuring reliability. These components are genuine, originally manufactured by the OEMs, and accompanied by interchangeability certificates, certificates of origin, and test certificates.

REPAIRS

We specialize in the repair of degraded and non-functional cards, offering component-level repairs or complete refurbishment. Our process takes into account factors such as aging, bulging, and overheating components, ensuring the restoration of cards to full functionality. All repairs come with a 12-month warranty.

TEST & CERTIFICATION

We ensure that all essential spare parts in your storeroom are prepared for immediate replacement. Our process involves testing and certifying the cards to guarantee their functional readiness when you pick them up.

BUY BACK & ASSET RECOVERY

Uncertain about handling downgraded parts or excess stock? Looking to convert unwanted inventory into more critical spares? We facilitate cross-buying between plants or can directly purchase the stock. Additionally, we conduct appraisals for unused stock slated for disposal, providing you with the designated value for the goods regardless of their depreciated value.
